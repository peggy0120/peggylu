Growth Stages of Grains

Designed for the "2014 Food x Production x Consumption Exhibition" at NTU Agricultural Exhibit Hall

Overview

  • The exhibition was aimed to introduce food education to elementary school and kindergarden students.
  • We used three iPads to showcase different growing stages of rice, wheat and millet respectively, in order to attract the kids' attention with the interactivity.
  • Swiping up and down or clicking the up/down arrows would show names and images of different stages.

Result

  • The parents at the exhibition that day thought the design was helpful for the learning.
