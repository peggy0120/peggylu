NTULIS IA Redesign

  • Wireframe
  • Team Project of Information Architecture & Web Design course (Spring 2014) at National Taiwan University (NTU)
  • Role:
    • Research - Benchmark, User Questionnaire
    • Planning - Cardsorting, Sitemap
    • Design - Making wireframe in Axure RP

Problem

The old website of the Department of Library & Information Science at NTU was built several years ago, and users had been having problems with finding relevent information.

Process

  • We first conducted website benchmarking of information architecture with 5 distinguished US iSchool website.
  • Before creating a student persona, we conducted questionnaire with NTULIS students, and interview with the department chairperson .
  • We used cardsorting to reorganize the structure, and later created the wireframe with Axure RP.

Result

  • Our presentation had received positive feedback in terms of the navigation and labeling from the class
  • The wireframe had provided a solid foundation for the later founded NTULIS Website Design Team
