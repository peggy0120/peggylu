NTULIS Course Schedule

Responsive course schedule page created with HTML5/CSS3/jQuery for the redesigned National Taiwan University Library & Information Science Department Website

Problem

  • The redesigned website needed a new page to showcase courses offered by the department that semester.
  • The questionnaire from the previous IA redesign project showed that "the courses" was the most used section by the students and they had hoped for an UI with better readability.

Challenge

  • Some certain jQuery codes would not work with Wordpress and other already installed plugins.
  • Needed to have the schedule page work with the responsive design of the new website on different systems and devices.

Result

  • It has recevied positive feedback from the faculty and students since first onlined in Summer 2014.
  • Currently in the process of turning it into a Wordpress plug-in.
